How it works

The recommendation is based on two different similarity measures between articles: the topic similarity and the authors collaborations similarity.

The final recommendation takes these metrics independently and a uniform linear combination between them, and retrieve the union of the top-10 neigbhors.

The topic similarity

Based on the content of the abstract, we extract the topics of the articles (i.e. what subject is it talking about). Articles sharing similar proportions of topics are assumed to be similar.

The authors collaborations similarity

The more authors articles share, the more similar they are.
